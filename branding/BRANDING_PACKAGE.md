## üìê Logo Usage Guidelines

### Clear Space
Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "S" in "SAYE" on all sides.

### Minimum Size
- **Digital:** 48x48px (app icon)
- **Print:** 1 inch / 2.54 cm (minimum width)

### Do's ‚úÖ
- Use official logo files from branding package
- Maintain original color palette
- Ensure sufficient contrast with background
- Scale proportionally (maintain aspect ratio)
- Use on white, light, or brand-colored backgrounds

### Don'ts ‚ùå
- Don't stretch or distort the logo
- Don't change logo colors (except approved variations)
- Don't add effects (shadows, glows, gradients to logo itself)
- Don't place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
- Don't rotate the logo at odd angles
- Don't separate emblem from text in full logo version

## üì¶ File Formats & Uses

### JPEG (.jpg)
**Current Format** - High quality, good compression
**Best for:** Photos, complex graphics, web use, social media
**Not ideal for:** Logos needing transparency

### PNG (Recommended for logos)
**Advantages:** Transparency support, lossless compression
**Best for:** Logos, icons, graphics needing transparent backgrounds
**Sizes:** 512x512, 1024x1024, 2048x2048

### SVG (Vector - Ideal for logos)
**Advantages:** Infinitely scalable, small file size, editable
**Best for:** Websites, print materials, professional design work
**Note:** Can be created from current JPEG designs
