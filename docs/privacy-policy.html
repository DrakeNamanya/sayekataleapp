1. Introduction

Welcome to SayeKatale ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our mobile application and services.

SayeKatale is an agricultural marketplace platform that connects Self-Help Group (SHG) farmers, Production and Supply Agents (PSA), and Small-Medium Enterprise (SME) buyers in Uganda. By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.
